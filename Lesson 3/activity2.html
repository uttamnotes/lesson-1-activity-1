Healthy Living — Quick Guide

Good habits can help us stay active, energetic, and healthy. Here is a quick guide using lists!

Steps for a Healthy Day:

  1. Wake up early and stretch for a few minutes
  2. Eat a nutritious breakfast
  3. Drink plenty of water throughout the day
  4. Spend some time exercising or playing outdoors
  5. Get enough sleep at night

Healthy Habits:

  • Eating fruits and vegetables every day
  • Taking short breaks from screens
  • Maintaining good posture while sitting
  • Washing hands regularly

Key Terms:

Nutrition
The process of obtaining and using food for growth and health
Exercise
Physical activity that helps keep the body fit and strong
Hydration
Maintaining the right amount of water in the body
