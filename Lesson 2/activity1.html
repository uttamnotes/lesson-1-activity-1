🚀 Journey Through Space

Welcome to an exciting adventure beyond Earth. Let's explore some fascinating facts about space!

🌕 Fact 1: The Moon

The Moon is Earth's only natural satellite and takes about 27 days to orbit our planet.

Why does the Moon shine?

The Moon does not produce its own light. It shines because it reflects sunlight back to Earth.

🪐 Fact 2: Saturn's Rings

Saturn is famous for its beautiful rings, which are made mostly of ice particles and rocky debris.

How many rings does Saturn have?

Saturn has seven main ring groups, each containing thousands of smaller ringlets.

Can we walk on Saturn?

No! Saturn is a gas giant and does not have a solid surface like Earth.

☀️ Fact 3: The Sun

The Sun contains about 99.8% of all the mass in our solar system and provides the energy needed for life on Earth.

How hot is the Sun?

The surface of the Sun is around 5,500°C, while its core can reach temperatures of about 15 million°C.

Fun comparison

More than one million Earths could fit inside the Sun.

Source

Facts sourced from NASA and the European Space Agency (ESA).
